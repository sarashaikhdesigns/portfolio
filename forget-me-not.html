Forget Me Not

10.2024

A multifaceted project inspired by flowers collected from urban landscapes, Forget Me Not explores themes of nature, memory, and artistic expression. The centerpiece of the project is a meticulously designed book featuring die-cut pages that replicate the delicate forms of the collected flowers, blending tactile design with botanical beauty. Complementing the book is a projection-based motion graphic, which brings the flowers to life in an immersive, dynamic display. The project also includes an interactive website, coded to enable users to create their own art pieces using the flower shapes as templates, fostering a participatory connection to the work. Together, these elements celebrate the ephemeral beauty of nature and its capacity to inspire creativity.
