Rituals of a Grieving Body

05.16.2026 (to be released)

"Rituals of a Grieving Body," my Parsons senior thesis, is a short film exploring the impact of technology on human connection and emotional intimacy. The project examines how digital spaces shape the way we process grief, presence, and relationships in an increasingly mediated world. Watch the trailer before the film’s release on May 16, 2026.
