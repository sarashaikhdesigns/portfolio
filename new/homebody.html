TYPOGRAPHIC DESIGN
Homebody Book

04.2024

A 16-page saddle-stitched book designed to guide readers in discovering a sense of home within themselves. Each page presents a visual narrative, inviting personal reflection on belonging and identity. Produced using a combination of risograph printing, inkjet printing, and laser cutting, the book merges tactile craftsmanship with meaningful storytelling, leaving a lasting impression on its audience.
