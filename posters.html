ABOUT DESIGN PHOTOGRAPHY
Polaris

02.2024

A cyanotype-printed artwork that uses a historical sun-dyeing process traditionally associated with blueprints, presented on a 24×36-inch canvas tapestry. The piece centers on the theme of navigation, incorporating asterisk motifs, symbolic illustrations, and layered annotations. Through its thoughtful integration of these elements, the work bridges historical significance with modern aesthetics, reflecting the enduring importance of guidance and direction.
